--- title: "The E-bike Dilemma" excerpt: "Analysis of E-bike driving behaviors at road intersections based on video surveillance data: a case study in Ningbo
" collection: portfolio ---

The disorderly driving behavior of electric bicycles at urban road intersections has been concerned by law enforcement departments, but there are few studies on the driving behavior rules of electric bicycles. Taking Jiangbei District of Ningbo City as an example, this study uses video surveillance data to classify and identify e-bikes and extract their trajectories, and uses spatial data analysis method to analyze the driving characteristics of e-bikes at road intersections and the influence of environmental factors on them.

This project is my undergraduate research project at Peking University, supported by the President's Fund of Peking University. This project is supervised by Prof. Xu Liyan.
